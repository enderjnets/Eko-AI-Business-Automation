# App Description for Yelp Fusion API

**Eko AI Business Automation** is an AI-powered sales automation platform for local business prospecting. Built for Denver, CO, it autonomously discovers leads, enriches them with AI research, and manages outreach across email, voice, and SMS.

We use the Yelp Fusion API to find qualified local businesses (restaurants, salons, contractors, etc.) within specific zip codes and cities. Yelp data — ratings, review counts, categories, and contact info — feeds our lead scoring engine and helps our AI agents personalize outreach. This integration directly supports small businesses looking to grow through targeted, automated sales campaigns.

**Primary use case**: Discovery and enrichment of local business leads.
**Data stored**: Business name, address, phone, category, rating, and review count (no consumer/reviewer data).
**Users**: Small business owners and sales teams in Colorado.
